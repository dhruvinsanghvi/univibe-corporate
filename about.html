    What we're all about

    Univibe network started off with this simple conversation to connect alumni and relive our college memories. As alums of a management institute in Mumbai, we understand leveraging the value of your alumni network can be one of the biggest challenges an institution faces.

    We make it easy for schools to have their own branded web and mobile platform that not only keeps your alumni engaged and connected, and offers professional and personal growth.

    By helping you unlock the full networking potential of your alumni database, we aim to build goodwill towards your organization, improve your alumni relations, and help you achieve your advancement goals.
